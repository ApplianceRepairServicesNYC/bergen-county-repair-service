Washer Repair Learn More

We Service All Types:

  • Front Load Washers
  • Top Load Washers
  • Commercial Washers
  • Residential Washers
  • Portable Washers
  • Washer/Dryer Combos
  • Stackable Washers

Professional washer repair services for all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Maytag, GE, Frigidaire, and more. Our certified technicians diagnose and repair all types of washing machine problems quickly and efficiently.

Common washer issues we repair include door boot seal leaks, bearing failures causing loud noises during spin cycles, drain pump failures, and control board malfunctions. We also fix lid switch failures, transmission problems, water inlet valve issues, and agitator problems.

Whether your washer won't drain, makes unusual noises, stops mid-cycle, or won't start at all, our experienced technicians can restore reliable operation. We carry common parts including lid switches, water inlet valves, and drain pumps for same-day repairs throughout Bergen County.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about appliance repair in Bergen County.

How quickly can you repair my appliance? +
We offer same-day service for most appliance repairs in Bergen County. When you call before noon, we can often schedule an appointment for later that same day. Our technicians arrive in fully-stocked service vehicles carrying hundreds of common parts, allowing us to complete approximately 90% of repairs during the first visit.
What brands do you repair? +
We repair all major appliance brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Maytag, GE, Frigidaire, KitchenAid, Bosch, Miele, Sub-Zero, Viking, Wolf, Thermador, Kenmore, Amana, and many more. Our technicians are trained on both domestic and imported appliances.
How much does appliance repair cost? +
Repair costs vary depending on the appliance type, specific problem, and parts required. We charge a diagnostic fee which is applied toward the repair cost if you proceed. After diagnosis, we provide a detailed written estimate before any work begins—no surprises or hidden fees.
What areas in Bergen County do you serve? +
We provide appliance repair service to all 70 municipalities in Bergen County, New Jersey. Our coverage includes Hackensack, Paramus, Teaneck, Fort Lee, Englewood, Ridgewood, Fair Lawn, Bergenfield, Mahwah, and all surrounding communities. View our complete service area list →
Do you offer emergency or weekend service? +
Yes, we offer service 7 days a week including weekends and holidays. Our regular hours are 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. For true emergencies—such as gas leaks, refrigerator failures, or flooding from appliance malfunctions—we provide priority response.
Do you offer any repair guarantees? +
Yes, all our repairs come with a comprehensive service warranty. Parts are covered by the manufacturer's warranty, and our labor is warranted for 90 days. If the same problem recurs within the warranty period, we'll return to fix it at no additional charge.
Should I repair or replace my appliance? +
The repair-versus-replace decision depends on the appliance age, repair cost, and overall condition. A general rule: if repair costs exceed 50% of replacement cost for an appliance over 8 years old, replacement may be wiser. We'll provide honest recommendations based on your specific situation.
