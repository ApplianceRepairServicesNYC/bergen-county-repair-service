Full-Service Repair for Every Home Appliance

We repair the complete range of household appliances, including refrigerators, freezers, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, cooktops, microwaves, and ventilation hoods. No matter which appliance is causing problems, our technicians bring the knowledge, tools, and parts needed to diagnose and repair it efficiently. This full-service approach saves Bergen County homeowners the hassle of finding separate specialists for different appliances.

Refrigerator repair is one of our most requested services, given how critical these appliances are to food safety and household function. We address all refrigerator problems, from cooling issues and ice maker failures to water leaks and compressor problems. Our technicians understand the sealed refrigeration system and can handle both simple fixes and complex repairs requiring specialized equipment.

Laundry appliance repair keeps Bergen County families' clothes clean and dry. We fix washers that won't spin, drain, or fill properly, as well as dryers that don't heat, tumble, or vent correctly. Safety is paramount with dryers, so we always check ventilation systems and advise customers on proper maintenance to prevent fire hazards.
